Projects

Our staff and associates have been responsible for helping clients validate their business needs, making improvement propositions,

and delivering business benefits, for example:

 

 

 

Environment Agency

Rewriting their M&E technology, materials and workmanship specifications.

Leading to: more sustainable, low energy, and smarter asset in the EA estate, with greater flexibility and inter-operability for the future.

 

 

 

Advanced Plasma Power

Help provided to draw-in investment and to work-up industry leading high efficiency solutions for making municipal solid waste a valuable resource.

Leading to: improved opportunities and advancement of advanced conversion technology solutions.

 

 

Anglian Water

Help provided to structure and setup an award winning capital delivery alliance; substantially improving the capital delivery performance and efficiency; helping to propel AWS to be one of the best performing UK water utilities. Using such skills as risk & value assessment, root cause evaluation and collaborative team building.

 

 

Accenture

Supporting Accenture in their development of Smart Water propositions; to take full advantage of disparate real-time data sets in order to optimise operational performance of assets. Also helping to create roadmaps for integrated programme management solutions for utilities.

 

 

GLA London and London Development Agency

With our associates, we developed and deployed demonstrator projects for solar PV, fuel cell CCHP, and EfW. Launched the ‘Better Buildings Partnership’ and piloted a 500 Green Companies scheme.

Leading to: driving lower carbon Cities (C40 Cities) to make cities more sustainable for the future.

 

 

EDF Energy

Part of their sustainable energy solutions and decentralised energy programme. Helping to maximise their position as a major low carbon energy provider in London and South East UK. Including deployment of combined heat & power (CHP) and district heating solutions; setting the way for what is now developing into multi-utility eco zones (such as Kings Cross, Elephant & Castle, Tower Hamlets, etc)
