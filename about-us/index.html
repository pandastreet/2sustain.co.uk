About Us

We are a small business operating in the asset intensive industries, established by an ex-Director of a major global engineering company in 2013 with a network of associates. Our core team has over 100 years experience.

Working in asset intensive infrastructure sectors, we help our clients:

  • Develop & assess business improvement opportunities
  • Opex reviews
  • Optimise asset investment performance
  • SCADA and telemetry strategy
  • Understand & address root cause issues
  • Data analytics for Insight
  • Capital Asset Investment prioritisation
  • Understand complex asset peformance data
  • Turning data into Insight into Action

In summary, we are helping asset intensive utilities and businesses, improve their risk, opex cost and capital asset strategy position.

Our mantra is: “We help to sustain and support your continued success, using our insight and innovation.”.
