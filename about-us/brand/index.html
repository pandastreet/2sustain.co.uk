Brand

2Sustain™ aims to live up to its name and projects a consistent brand:

  • To use sustainable thinking to drive better business outcomes
  • To use the 2Sustain™ Value Tower framework
  • To sustain us all
  • To have people and property that exist by 2Sustain principles
  • All our energy comes from Ecotricity™ which is > 50% renewables
  • All our staff to use less than 110 litres per capita per day of potable water
  • Wherever possible travel is done using low carbon means (eg. Rail or avoided by remote conferencing)
  • To sustain and promote collaboration and innovation as tools for business improvement
  • For us, Purple is the new Green.
